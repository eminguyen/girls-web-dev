What are the different starter Pokemon?

(Gen 1 only because the other generations suck.)

For generations people have been arguing what is the best starter Pokemon. Is it Charmander or is it Squirtle? This website will compare the different features between the Pokemon and help you decide which one to start your journey with!

Charmander

This is Charmander. He is a fire lizard with an orange body, blue eyes, and fangs. A fire burns at the tip of his tail. If this flame goes out, it's rumored that Charmander will die.



Why is Charmander the best?

  • He is a lizard that breathes fire
  • He evolves into a freaking dragon
  • Objectively the coolest looking starter Pokemon

What's wrong with Charmander?

  • The first two gym leaders you battle are rock and water. You're going to get destroyed
  • Can only learn useless HM's like Cut
  • In the anime, Charizard was a real jerk for several episodes

Squirtle

This is Squirtle. He is a blue turtle that spits out water from his mouth. His tail curls inward. He will often use his shell in battle to protect himself (Unfortunately, Withdraw is the most useless move in the video games despite how cool it looks in the anime).



Why is Squirtle the best?

  • He can match Charmander in coolness when he's in the Squirtle Squad
  • He evolves into a giant tortoise with cannons on its back
  • He can learn an actually useful HM - Surf
